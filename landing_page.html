FORM AND DEFINITION


Synthwave is a musical and aesthetic niche with origins in the mid-2000s with influences from movie soundtracks, video games and elevision between the 80s and 90s.


Aesthetically, synthwave gained a retrofuturism perspective, being also called retrowave, emulating 80s science fiction and action scenes. Examples of aesthetics seen in movies and video games are: F-Zero, In Time, Blade Runner, Drive, Grand Theft Auto: Vice City, Hotline Miami and Far Cry 3: Blood Dragon.


As the genre draws from diverse influences, there is a wide variety of structures used in the songs, allowing artists a wide creative spectrum. This characteristic gave rise to a countless number of subgenres (in some moments, the distinction between them occurs more by differences in aesthetic orientation than by music). Just to the right (or below if you're using a smartphone) you can hear one of my favorite variations: the chillsynth. Please feel free to enjoy the experience through the work of one of the best artists today A.L.I.S.O.N
